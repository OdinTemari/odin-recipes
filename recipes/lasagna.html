Lasagna Recipe

Here are your recipes to make a beautiful home made lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 2 pkt Spagheti
  • 1/2Lb Onion
  • 100Lb Stamina

    • Steps

    Follow these simple steps to make the lasagna to your test

      1. Collect your stamina, and get it rolling.
      2. Cut and chop the onions to manageable size.
      3. Put on stove, and let them cook until graduation.
